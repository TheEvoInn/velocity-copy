# Digital Resellers Quick Start

## 🚀 Get Started in 5 Minutes

### Step 1: Open Digital Resellers Department
1. Click **DigitalResellers** in the left sidebar
2. You'll see the main dashboard with key metrics

### Step 2: Launch Autopilot
1. Click the large **"Launch Autopilot Reseller"** button
2. System initializes your configuration
3. Automated scanning begins immediately

### Step 3: Wait for Opportunities
1. Opportunities will appear in 5-10 minutes
2. Go to **Opportunities** tab
3. You'll see discovered resale opportunities

### Step 4: Generate Your First Page
1. Click **Generate Page** on any opportunity
2. AI handles everything automatically:
   - Writes headlines and copy
   - Creates product descriptions
   - Generates images
   - Sets up payment processing
   - Creates lead forms
3. Page is published instantly

### Step 5: Monitor & Earn
1. Go to **Storefronts** tab
2. Watch real-time metrics:
   - Revenue per page
   - Conversion rates
   - Visitor traffic
   - Customer count

## 💻 Configuration Options

### Identity & Branding
Go to **Configuration** tab to:
- Select which identity the pages use
- Verify brand colors applied
- Check fonts and typography
- Confirm tone of voice

### Automation Settings
Toggle these options:
- **Auto Generate Pages** - Automatically create pages for opportunities
- **Auto Publish** - Immediately publish pages (vs. review first)
- **Email Notifications** - Get alerts on new sales
- **Analytics Tracking** - Track visitor behavior

### Goals
Set your targets:
- **Monthly Revenue Goal** - Target income amount
- **Max Pages** - Concurrent pages limit
- **Scan Frequency** - How often to search opportunities
- **Content Mode** - AI-driven or template-based

**Q: How much does this cost?**
A: Standard platform fees apply. Page generation is included.

**Q: Can I manually edit pages?**

A: First sales typically within 3-7 days of publishing.
